My Latest Work


The Dual Dimension – Album Cover / Vinyl Record

May 2025

This is a vinyl record design I created as a personal project, inspired by themes of duality and contrast. two human silhouettes form a yin-yang shape, representing balance and connection. The record label design is clean and minimal to contrast with the busy cover, keeping the focus on the music and track list. I aimed to blend retro vinyl vibes with a modern graphic style.

ROAM Magazine – Social Media Content

February 2025

This is a concept project for ROAM Magazine, a made-up publication focused on van life, road adventures, and DIY living. I designed a series of Instagram posts to promote an issue of the magazine, I wanted the visuals to feel warm, adventurous, and down-to-earth just like the stories themselves. I used a topographic map pattern in the background, earthy colors, and clean, bold typography to keep everything consistent and easy to follow. The goal was to create a scroll-worthy, on-brand social media presence.

Crystal Roller Series – Email + Mobile Promo Design

July 2024

A responsive email design concept for a fictional wellness brand, The Crystal Ball. The goal was to promote a new crystal roller series with a soft, calming aesthetic. I designed both desktop and mobile versions, focusing on clean layout, soothing visuals, and a clear path to shop.
